Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

CaloriEat (this web application) is designed so that your nutrition data stays on your device using browser storage (typically localStorage). We do not operate a user database or cloud sync in this version.

What We Collect

  • Information you enter: Stored locally in your browser (e.g., profile fields, meals, weigh-ins, goals).
  • Technical data: Your browser and hosting provider may collect standard logs (IP, user agent) when you load the site—outside CaloriEat’s code.
  • Third-party resources: Fonts or Chart.js may be loaded from CDNs; those providers may see requests per their own policies.

What We Do Not Do (This Build)

  • No account recovery server, no marketing email list, and no sale of personal data through this app.
  • Contact/demo forms, if shown, may not transmit messages until a backend is connected.

Your Choices

Clear site data in your browser to remove local profiles. Use private/incognito mode if you prefer not to persist data between sessions.

Contact

Questions: use the contact page. For legal notices, use the same channel until a dedicated address is published.
